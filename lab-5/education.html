My Education

Elementary School

School One Elementary School

I attended School One Elementary School from 2001-2006 in Scotch Plains, New Jersey. It was the best school in the country because it was the only elementary school with air conditioning. My favorite thing about elementary school was the Halloween Fair every October. My favorite class was Art.



Middle School

Park Middle School

I attended Park Middle School for one year, 2006-2007, and I hated it. Every was mean and I was too nerdy, so I transferred to another school. I did not have a favorite class.



Middle School Part 2

Wardlaw Hartridge School

I transferred to a private school and loved it. Located in Edison, NJ Wardlaw Hartridge was a K-12 school, and we all wore uniforms (I was really into vests). I was first flute in the band and participated all-state choir, which was my favorite part. My favorite class was music.



High School

Academy for Information Technology

For high school I went to a Magnet School, Union County Vocational Technical School. The particular school I went to was for Computer Science. I got certified in A+, Oracle, and Microsoft Word, Excel, and Powerpoint. I learned a lot in high school, but there was no music in my school. I ended up joining a choir outside of class, and performed at Carnegie Hall my senior year. I am in touch with all of my best friends from High school, and 2 go to Stevens with me. My favorite class was Ethics.



College

Stevens Institute of Technology

I am a senior Music & Technology Major with a Computer Science minor here at Stevens. I have had a great 4 years, and I am sad it is ending. In my time here I am a part of the Stevens band, orchestra, choir, president's ensemble, Alpha Phi Kappa Delta Chapter, a peer mentor, and a global ambassador. I am the current President of the Stevens Computer Science Club. My favorite memory at Stevens has been meeting some of my best friends, and getting to perform every week in all of my ensembles. My favorite class was MIDI and Electronic Music.



College - STUDY ABROAD

University of Westminster

My summer of my sophomore year I studied abroad in London, at the Univeristy of Westminster, and it was the best 3 months of my life. I travelled to different countries each weekend and learned a lot about how to be independent in unfarmiliar places. One of my favorite memories is sight seeing through the English countryside, it is beautiful! My favorite class was London through it's Museums.
